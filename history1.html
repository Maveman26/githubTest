Building on the experience gained in building the Dallas Innovation Alliance

cross-sector consortium, including the fastest-to-market smart cities initiative

in the country, there was always a recognition that a regional, cross-

jurisdictional approach to innovation, resilience and smart cities was critical.

The North Texas Innovation Alliance takes the success of the DIA model and

scales it to support the North Texas region.
